AI Engineering — Architecture Reference

An end-to-end reference for the modern AI stack: building Mini PyTorch, Mini vLLM, Mini LangChain, Mini Hugging Face, and a Mini Research Lab from scratch.

  • Mini PyTorch — Autograd, MatMul, Softmax, Transformer, Flash Attention, ViT, Diffusion
  • Mini vLLM — Inference server, KV cache, speculative decoding, quantization
  • Mini LangChain — CoT, ReAct, RAG, HNSW, function calling, Graph RAG
  • Mini Hugging Face — BPE, LoRA, PEFT, eval harness, model merger
  • Mini Research Lab — RLHF/PPO, DPO, distillation, synthetic data, MoE, FSDP
